Safety Guidelines for Buying Second Hand Bikes

  • Inspect the bike thoroughly for any visible damage or wear, especially on the frame and brakes.
  • Ensure the brakes, tires, and gears are in working condition before making a purchase.
  • Check for original documents and proof of ownership to avoid issues later on.
  • Always wear safety gear like a helmet when riding a bike.
